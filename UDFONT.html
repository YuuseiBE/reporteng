Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is there a fee to use this site?

    A: No, using this site is free.

  • Q: Can you tell me more about the bot's features?

    A: The bot provides reporting and question functions, sending appropriate information to the administrators.

  • Q: How are the reports handled?

    A: The reports are reviewed by the administrators and appropriate actions are taken.

  • Q: Can I receive a response to my question?

    A: Yes, responses to your questions will be sent through Discord.

  • Q: What should I do if I want to provide additional information after submitting a report?

    A: Please resend the relevant information through the report or question form. The initial submission will be invalidated.

  • Q: Where can I check the terms of use?

    A: The terms of use can be checked here.

  • Q: How is the data on this site protected?

    A: We take appropriate measures to ensure the protection and privacy of data.

  • Q: I want to report a problem or bug on the site.

    A: Please inform us of any problems or bugs through the question form. We will address them as soon as possible.

  • Q: Will there be a delay in receiving a response to my report or question?

    A: Typically, responses or actions will be taken within 24 hours, but depending on the situation, it may take longer.

  • Q: Is there a way to withdraw a submitted report or question?

    A: Yes, you can withdraw by notifying us through the question form.

  • Q: Are there any limitations on what I can report?

    A: Reports should be relevant to the bot's operation. Irrelevant or inappropriate content may be disregarded.

  • Q: How do I contact support?

    A: Support can be contacted through the question form or via our official Discord server.

  • Q: Is the bot available in multiple languages?

    A: Currently, the bot primarily supports English, but we are working on adding more languages in the future.
